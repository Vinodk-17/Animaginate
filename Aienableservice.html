Power Platform: AI Enabled Services

COPILOT STUDIO

Copilot studio is a capability within the Power Platform that allows users to build, customize and deploy AI agents.

  • Agents can handle conversational interactions, knowledge retrieval, task execution and workflows
  • Agents can take user input, request/upload documents, extract/classify relevant information and handle validation (e.g., asking user for clarifications)
  • Agents can trigger workflows to share data, store data, or request approvals
Status: Copilot Studio is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
EMBEDDED COPILOTS

Copilot drafts contextual answers to queries in both chat and email, in addition to providing an interactive chat experience over knowledge bases and case history.

  • Copilot in Dynamics 365 customer service offers the following capabilities:
  • Summarize a case and conversation
  • Respond to questions
  • Compose an email
  • Ask a question about knowledge base sources and trusted domains
Status: Copilot for D365 is under review for foundational approval at Morgan Stanley
POWER APPS – PLAN DESIGNER

Plan designer is a copilot-first development tool that lets you quickly create comprehensive business solutions.

  • Describe your business use case in natural language and add relevant images, like business process flows or screenshots of legacy apps
  • Plan designer generates a complete Power Platform solution tailored to your needs.
Status: Plan designer is not yet GA at Morgan Stanley
AI BUILDER – PROMPTS

AI Builder is a feature within the Power Platform that allows users to easily add AI capabilities into their workflows, apps and processes.

  • AI Builder is a feature within the Power Platform that allows users to easily add AI capabilities into their workflows, apps and processes
  • AI Builder contains ready-made models, and lets you build and train custom models within your own
  • Examples include classifying documents into mortgages vs. credit card apps, or extract custom entities from forms
Status: AI Builder is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
