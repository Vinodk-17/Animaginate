COPILOT STUDIO
Copilot studio is a capability within the Power Platform that allows users to build, customize and deploy AI agents.
  • Handle conversational interactions, knowledge retrieval, task execution and workflows
  • Upload documents, extract and classify information, validate inputs
  • Trigger workflows to share data, store data or request approvals
Status: Copilot Studio is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
EMBEDDED COPILOTS
Copilot drafts contextual answers to queries in chat and email, providing an interactive experience over knowledge bases and case history.
  • Summarize cases and conversations
  • Respond to questions
  • Compose emails and query knowledge sources
Status: Copilot for D365 is under review for foundational approval at Morgan Stanley
POWER APPS – PLAN DESIGNER
Plan designer is a copilot-first development tool that lets you quickly create comprehensive business solutions.
  • Describe business use cases in natural language and add relevant images
  • Generates a complete Power Platform solution tailored to your needs
Status: Plan designer is not yet GA at Morgan Stanley
AI BUILDER – PROMPTS
AI Builder allows users to easily add AI capabilities into workflows, apps and business processes.
  • Ready-made models and custom model training
  • Document classification and entity extraction
Status: AI Builder is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
