COPILOT
STUDIO
Copilot studio is a capability within the Power Platform that allows users to build, customize and deploy AI agents.
  • Agents can handle conversational interactions, knowledge retrieval, task execution and workflows
  • Agents can take user input, request/upload documents, extract/classify relevant information and handle validation
  • Agents can trigger workflows to share data, store data, or request approvals
Status: Copilot Studio is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
EMBEDDED
COPILOTS
Copilot drafts contextual answers to queries in both chat and email, in addition to providing an interactive chat experience over knowledge bases and case history.
  • Copilot in Dynamics 365 customer service offers:
  • Summarize a case and conversation
  • Respond to questions
  • Compose an email
  • Ask about KB sources and trusted domains
Status: Copilot for D365 is under review for foundational approval at Morgan Stanley

PLAN DESIGNER

AI BUILDER –
PROMPTS
AI Builder is a feature within the Power Platform that allows users to easily add AI capabilities into their workflows, apps and processes.
  • Add AI capabilities into workflows, apps and processes
  • Ready-made models and ability to train custom models
  • Examples: document classification, entity extraction from forms
Status: AI Builder is currently approved and available for Pro Dev use cases at Morgan Stanley
